What is .EML?

.EML is a file type that allows this website to read information about your email. Such as where the email was sent from, where it has been, what type of attachments have been sent, etc. You can download a .EML file from Outlook by dragging the email from your inbox and onto your desktop. Then upload the .EML file to this website to analyze it. Outlook may also save the email as a .MSG file, which is supported too.
